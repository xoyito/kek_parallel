This is the Q'eqchi'-English Parallel Corpus
The corpus contains Q'eqchi' text scraped from 2148 URLs and English text scraped from 2148 URLs.
Lists of URLS that were scraped can be found in the Scraped directory as english_links.txt and kek_links.txt.


The full scraped text can be found in the Scraped directory, a script called parallel.py lines up the sentences that can be easily alligned into the files in the corpus directory.
The parallel sentences are available in plain text and .json format in the Corpus directory.

The parallel corpus contains:

	17043 parallel sentences.

	515661 English words and 19186 unique English tokens.

	653457 Q'eqchi' words and 23276 unique Q'eqchi' tokens.
